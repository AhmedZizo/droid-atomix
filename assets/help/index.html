    • Game Overview


    DroidAtomix is a puzzle game to build complex molecules from simple atoms. Move the atoms around the board to construct the goal molecule in each level. Atoms can move in any direction (up, down, right, left) unless they are obstructed by something (wall, or another atom). Atoms will move in the selected direction until they collide with a wall or another atom. You must be strategic with your movements in order to build the complex molecules.

    Levels

    There are 19 levels -- you will start at Level 1, but you may skip to any other level at any time. Use Menu to click "Levels" to see all levels.

    In the "Levels" window you can view all levels, and see your completion time and number of moves for levels you have completed. You may also redo a completed level to try to get a better score.

    Game Controls


    The simplest way to play is by clicking on atoms, then clicking on the arrows that appear around them, to move in a certain direction.

    Other Controls

    You may also use:
  • the D-Pad on devices like the Motorola Droid; or
  • the Trackball on devices like the HTC Dream or HTC Hero


    • When using these controls, move the red highlighted area around the board, then press the trackpad or the center button on the D-Pad to select an atom or arrow.

    Once you have used an arrow to move an atom, you can Undo your last move from the Menu.

    You can also see a larger view of the goal molecule from the Menu, or restart the current level you are playing.
